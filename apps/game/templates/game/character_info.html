These are the characters of "Game to be Named"

Knight

A heavily defended and physically powerful fighter, who is slow moving but deals and takes attacks well.

Mage

A powerful fighter who can attack from range using magical spells, but suceptible to attacks when fighinting at close range.

Rogue

A powerful fighter who excels at map mobility and one on one combat, but can be overwhelmed by fighting multiple foes at once.

Bard

A weaker fighter who excels defensively and can charm enemies into submission by playing songs from a magical harp.
